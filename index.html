FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before joining

How do I get admission?

Simply visit our library with a passport-size photo and ID proof. Fill out the registration form, choose your membership plan, and start studying the same day! You can also call us or fill the inquiry form on our website.

What are the library timings?

We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week! Yes, you read that right. Study anytime — early morning, late night, or weekends. Our staff is always present for your assistance.

Can I pay fees in installments?

Yes! For half-yearly and yearly plans, we offer easy installment options. Pay 50% upfront and the rest within 30 days. Contact us for more details on flexible payment plans.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking area for both two-wheelers and four-wheelers with a security guard. Parking is free for half-yearly and yearly members. Monthly members can park at a nominal charge of ₹100/month.

Do you provide study material?

We provide high-speed WiFi for accessing online study resources. While we don't provide physical books, our digital resource centre includes access to e-books, video lectures, and practice test portals for competitive exams.

Can I get a refund if I leave early?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee from the date of admission. After that, refunds are processed on a pro-rata basis minus a small processing fee. Please read our terms and conditions for complete details.
